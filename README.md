# Amaely website

One self contained file. Open `index.html` in a browser or host it anywhere
(GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel). Everything, including the 3D, is inside this file.

## What it is
A calm, premium site in your navy and cream brand. It opens on a deep navy hero with a
living "universe" of glowing blue and white points, then keeps only the useful parts:
what we do, the seven parts of health we look after (with the 3D), how it works, why families
trust us, real stories, and one clear way to get in touch.

It is now for everyone, not only parents. The words speak to you, your parents and the
people you love, in Tier 1, 2 and 3 towns and cities across India.

## The 3D
In the "What we care for" section the point cloud gently reshapes as you scroll, becoming a
mind, a heart, nourishment, a strand of DNA, rest, protection and the whole self, one for
each line. A soft dark halo sits behind the words so the text always stays readable over the
animation. Orange appears only on the main buttons and as a rare spark in the stars.

## The people
The faces in "Who we care for" and in "Stories" are friendly illustrations, not real people.
I cannot use real photos of real people, so these are warm, diverse stand ins with gentle
Indian cues. Swap them for real, consented photos when you have them.

## Important: the stories are samples
The testimonials are made up, only to show how the section looks. There is a small note on
the page saying so. Please do not publish them as real. Replace each quote, name and photo
with a real family's words and image, with their permission, before you go live.

- Colours: navy #002855, slate blue #78A5B9, cream #F0EBE6, white, orange #F06437 used sparingly.
- Your brand fonts are Utile Display and Neue Haas Unica. Those are licensed and cannot be
  embedded, so the site lists them first and falls back to Hanken Grotesk for headlines and
  Helvetica Neue for body. Add the licensed files later and it switches automatically.
